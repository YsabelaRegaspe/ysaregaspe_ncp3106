JACK EN POY

Game Description:

Get ready to play "Jack en Poy," the classic game of rock, paper, scissors with a twist! In this game,
you'll be presented with five buttons: rock, paper, scissors, reveal, and new game.

The game secretly generates its own pick—either rock, paper, or scissors—but won't reveal it right away.
Your task is to choose between rock, paper, and scissors by clicking the corresponding button. After making
your choice, click the "Reveal" button to see what your opponent has picked.

The outcome will be instantly displayed, letting you know if you've won or lost the round. If you want to
play again, simply click the "New Game" button to start fresh and take another shot at victory!

IN-BETWEEN GAME

Game Description:

Welcome to the thrilling "In-Between Game," a test of your luck and decision-making. Start by choosing
your number of rounds—3, 5, or 10—which determines your starting money. After selecting, the game deals
two cards, and you must place a bet before deciding whether to go with "Deal" or "No Deal." If the cards
are not identical, choosing "Deal" means you'll win if the third card falls between the first two, while
"No Deal" forfeits 10% of your money without further risk. When faced with identical cards, you'll need to
decide if the third card will be higher or lower.

Match all three cards, and you hit the JACKPOT, tripling your bet! Ready to test your luck? Click "New Game"
and see if you can beat the odds! Each decision you make could bring you closer to victory, or you might have
to make tough choices to hold on to your money. Play smart, and you might just walk away with a big win!
